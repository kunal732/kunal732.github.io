What is SendGrid Accelerate ?

SendGrid Accelerate is our new initiative designed to help Startups succeed. We've been there ourselves and understand what it takes and the tools,resources startups need to really take off. We have a team dedicated to helping your company succeed. Startups that are a part of our accelerate program get technical and business mentorship from our Developer Relations team, a healthly dose of SendGrid credits, top of the funnel marketing and anything else we can do to provide assistance.
